About Us

At Little Cubs English Medium Day Care in Mosate, Mokopane, we provide a safe, nurturing, and fun environment for children. Our aim is to ensure that our Little Cubs are obedient, respectful, and confident while learning and playing in English.

Our Programs

Infants (3m – 1y)

Gentle care, sensory activities, and early language exposure. No screen time.

Toddlers (1 – 3y)

Interactive learning, arts & crafts, music, and movement.

Pre-schoolers (3 – 5y)

Literacy, numeracy, storytelling, and outdoor play activities.
