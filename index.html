About Us

At Little Cubs English Medium Day Care we create a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can grow, learn, and have fun. Our dedicated team of caregivers and educators focus on fostering curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every child. At Little Cubs, we emphasize respect, kindness, and positive behavior, ensuring that each little learner develops essential social, emotional, and cognitive skills while enjoying a variety of engaging activities in English. We are committed to laying a strong foundation for lifelong learning, helping our Little Cubs thrive both inside and outside the classroom.

Our Uniforms

Our uniform is designed to be comfortable, practical, and fun for the children. Each Little Cub wears our specially designed uniform that reflects our bright, playful, and welcoming environment. Uniforms help create a sense of belonging, encourage discipline, and make it easy for parents to prepare their children for school each day.

Our Programs

Infants (3m – 1y)

Gentle care, sensory activities, and early language exposure. No screen time.

Toddlers (1 – 3y)

Interactive learning, arts & crafts, music, and movement.

Pre-schoolers (3 – 5y)

Literacy, numeracy, storytelling, and outdoor play activities.
